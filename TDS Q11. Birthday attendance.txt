11. Given the following two tables, write a query to return the fraction of students, rounded to 
two decimal places, who attended school
(attendance = 1) on their birthday.

Method 1--------------------------------
Select ROUND (count (distinct case when year_mon_school_date= date_of_birth and attendance=1 then student_id else NULL end) / count ( distinct student_id) ,2) as attendance_pct 
FROM
(
Select student_id, concat(extract(year from school_date), “-“, extract(month from school_date)) as year_mon_school_date, attendance, 
concat(extract(year from date_of_birth), “-“, extract(month from date_of_birth)) as year_mon_ date_of_birth
From attendance join students
On attendance.student_id= students.student_id
) t1
